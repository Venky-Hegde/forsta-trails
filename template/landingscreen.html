forstais a digital assistant for staffing,
forsta makes staffing process efficient in catering to multiple customers and their staffing needs.
forsta significantly reduces use of email which is extensively used for interaction.It reduces the frequency of trackers that are exchanged and profiles that are sent back and forth.
It also provides the ability for staffing companies to create and manage thier repository of profiles and easily match with job and share with companies.
The process of making forsta deliver value incrementally is an ongoing process and your feedback is well appreciated!
Key features
Staffing Agencies
  • Manage multiple customers
  • View Jobs/Attach Profiles
  • View Status
  • Converse with recruiters
  • Build profile repository
Companies
  • Create Jobs
  • View profiles shared
  • View Status
  • Converse with staffing agencies
  • Update status
